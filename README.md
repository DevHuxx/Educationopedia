## 📌 About

Educationopedia is a modern overseas education platform focused on helping Indian students pursue **MBBS abroad** in **NMC & WHO-approved universities**.

The platform provides complete guidance for students — from counseling and university selection to visa processing and admission support.

---

# ✨ Features

- 🎯 MBBS Abroad Counseling  
- 🏥 NMC & WHO Approved Universities  
- 📄 Admission Assistance  
- 🛂 Visa Guidance & Documentation
